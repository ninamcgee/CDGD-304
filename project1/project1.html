bard coffee rebrand

Bard Coffee is a coffee shop located in the artistic downtown of Portland, Maine.
Bard's mission is to make every drink feel hand crafted. For my branding class at
MassArt I recreated Bard's current logo into something that reflects their mission.
The logo I created is a hand made contour drawing along with handmade type.
I also created a set of hand drawn patterns to pair with the logo.
